Eduardo Torres-Jara

Eduardo is the president of Robot Rebuilt. He was formerly an assistant professor at WPI, and a postdoctoral associate in the Distributed Robotics Laboratory at MIT and the Microrobotics Laboratory at Harvard University. He was founder and CTO of the Telecommunications Division of Autotrack, and founder and CTO of Enkasoft.
